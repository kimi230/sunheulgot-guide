Before Check-in

Before You Arrive

Door Code

The door code is the last 4 digits of your phone number.

Check-in / Check-out

Check-in is after 3:00 PM, check-out is by 11:00 AM.

Heating & Hot Water

The 2nd floor uses electric heating. The 1st floor heating and hot water use LPG gas.

Conservation

Please help conserve water and energy, considering Jeju's water scarcity and energy costs.

No Parties

Parties are strictly prohibited after 10:00 PM.

After Check-in

After You Arrive

Bedding

We've prepared bedding for the queen bed and the number of guests you requested. For 2 guests: queen bed only. For 3: one extra futon set. For 4: two extra futon sets. No additional bedding is available after check-in.

Wi-Fi

Scan the QR code printed on top of the router (located on the shoe cabinet), or check the network name and password on the bottom of the router. (KT)

Waste Disposal

Please bring your waste and recyclables to the 'Seonheul 1-ri Recycling Center (Seonheul-ri 2541-1)'. A staff member there will kindly help you sort everything.

View Map

Do Not Leave Trash Outside

Never leave trash outside the house. Please always bring it to the recycling center. Birds and cats will tear the bags open immediately.

Caution

Cautions & House Notes

Safety & Rules

Loft Stairs

This is a loft-style accommodation. Please be careful when using the stairs.

2nd Floor Hammock

The hammock on the 2nd floor is not for children. Only one adult at a time.

LIABILITY

Safety Accidents

We are not liable for accidents caused by guest negligence.

About Our Home

Pets

Please keep your pets in the yard and 1st floor only. For safety and hygiene, please do not allow them on the 2nd floor.

No TV

There is no TV in our home. We hope you can fully immerse yourself in Jeju's nature and enjoy some quality time for yourself.

Barbecue

Feel free to use the grill from the storage shed for barbecue. (Please bring your own charcoal and grill grate.)

Dryer

There is a storage shed behind the winter building. Open the door and you'll see the dryer right away. Feel free to use it.
